Features

Connects directly to your databases. No cloud proxy, no data leaving your machine, no subscription.

Multi-Database Support

Connect to 8+ vector databases. Manage multiple named connections with encrypted credential storage.

Advanced Visualizations

Interactive 2D/3D scatter plots with UMAP, t-SNE, PCA dimensionality reduction and K-means/DBSCAN clustering.

Embedding Functions

Write custom JavaScript embedding functions. Templates for OpenAI, Cohere, Hugging Face, and a local test server.

Privacy First

Local-first architecture with HTTPS/TLS and encrypted credentials. Your data never leaves your machine.

Distance Metrics

Cosine Similarity, Euclidean Distance, and Dot Product. Choose the right metric for your data.

Vector & Hybrid Search

Similarity search with metadata filters, result comparison, and BM25/lexical search on supported databases.
